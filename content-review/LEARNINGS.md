## Standing Rules

1. Every video MUST have a thumbnail — do not generate a final without one.
2. Vary background music across videos — do not reuse the same track consecutively.
3. Voice-over must start within the first 1–2 seconds — no silent gap at the start.
4. Check all rejections above before generating a new video for a previously-rejected topic.
